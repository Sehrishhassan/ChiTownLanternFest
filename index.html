ABOUT THIS FESTIVAL

The Chi Town Lantern Fest, thousands of revelers join together armed with lanterns for one unforgettable spectacle. There will be music, dancing, s’mores and, when the time is just right, we will light the sky with our highest hopes, deepest regrets, and fondest dreams.

WHAT YOU'LL NEED

It can get a bit chilly during our event. Make sure to bring proper clothing. Feel free to bring your own folding chair or blankets to relax. And don’t forget your camera.
